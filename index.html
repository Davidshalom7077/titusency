Our Schedule

Meet Happy Couple

The wedding ceremony will be held at the C.S.I. Christ Church, a place where we will solemnize our commitment to each other in the presence of God. The church, with its timeless beauty and serene atmosphere, will serve as the perfect backdrop for this sacred moment when we promise to walk hand in hand through the journey of life. We are looking forward to sharing this significant moment with you, our cherished family and friends, as your support and presence mean the world to us. Following the church ceremony, we will gather for a traditional wedding feast at Aruna Thirumana Mandabam, Kalakad. The feast will be a grand celebration of love, joy, and togetherness, filled with delicious food, laughter, and beautiful memories. This will be the time to come together, celebrate our new beginning, and share in the happiness of the occasion. We hope that you will join us in this wonderful celebration, and we look forward to enjoying this special meal with all of you. In addition to the wedding ceremony and feast, we would also like to invite you to a groom's reception that will take place at S.J Mahal, Parappadi. This event will be an opportunity for more festivities and celebrations, where we can spend time with each of you, extend our gratitude for your support, and make more wonderful memories to cherish forever. The reception will be a lively gathering with music, dance, and joy as we mark the beginning of our new life together with the love and blessings of our family and friends. We hope that you will join us for these wonderful celebrations. Your presence at our wedding will bring us great joy, and we would be honored to have you share in the excitement of this new chapter in our lives. We are excited to have our loved ones witness this significant moment, and we know that having you with us will make it all the more meaningful and memorable.
